## 🎯 The Hook
[cite_start]Your clients are flooding in on GLP-1 medications like Ozempic, Wegovy, and Mounjaro[cite: 3]. ## 💼 Business Strategies
* [cite_start]**Package a "GLP-1 Strong" Program**: Stop selling standard training; create a specialized companion program focused on "muscle preservation"[cite: 4].
* [cite_start]**Automate Plateau Check-Ins**: Clinical trials show weight loss plateaus around the 60-week mark[cite: 5]. [cite_start]Automate your CRM to flag clients at the 9-to-12-month timeline to shift focus toward progressive overload[cite: 5].

## 🧠 Coaching Concepts
* [cite_start]**Scheduled Hydration Protocol**: GLP-1 drugs suppress thirst signals[cite: 6]. [cite_start]Implement a strict strategy aiming for 3.7 liters daily with electrolyte supplementation[cite: 8].
